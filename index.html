Welcome!

EthicAlly is a research ethics support system for the social sciences and humanities, powered by AI. Enter your research proposal and receive a free structured report outlining potential ethical issues and making suggestions to address them. The report is generated by Claude Sonnet 4, a Large Language Model made by Anthropic. Please note that EthicAlly cannot review clinical research.

CAUTION: EthicAlly is a research app and still under development. It can make mistakes. Always seek ethical advice from your institutional research ethics committee or equivalent.
